About Us

The Atlantis Venture is an innovative initiative focused on leveraging technology to explore, protect, and sustain marine ecosystems. Comprised of a dedicated team of innovators, conservationists, and visionaries, the organization aims to revolutionize marine preservation with advanced tools and platforms, such as AquaSnap, which is designed for species identification. Committed to impactful educational programs and conservation technologies, The Atlantis Venture emphasizes the importance of marrying innovation with responsibility to foster a sustainable and thriving marine future.

Start Up Features

AquaSnap is an innovative mobile application designed to revolutionize the way we identify and explore marine life. Equipped with powerful image recognition technology, AquaSnap allows users to easily identify marine species with just a snap of a photo.
